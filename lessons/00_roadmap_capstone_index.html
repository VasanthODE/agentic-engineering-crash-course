One production agent, all eight lessons

Every concept lives in a real, containerized customer-support agent — the AcmeCorp Support Assistant. The roadmap isn't theory; it's a map of this codebase.

Build it. Ship it. Know it works.

That's the whole arc. Lessons 1–4 build the agent's mind — reasoning, memory, and the loop that lets it act. Lesson 5 connects it to the world. Lessons 6–7 are the infrastructure and safety that turn a demo into something you can put in front of real users. Lesson 8 is how you know — and keep knowing — that it works.

You didn't learn eight tools. You learned how the pieces compose into a system, why each exists, and what breaks without it. That's the difference between using agents and engineering them.
